STANDOFFISH LLC

We are a small and deliberate atelier of design: a studio not of volume, but of measure. Our practice inclines toward the arcane virtues—proportion, restraint, cadence—wherein a form is wrought by subtraction and made credible by silence. We labor at the margins of the obvious, serving those who prefer the austere to the ornate, the exact to the loud, the enduring to the immediate.
